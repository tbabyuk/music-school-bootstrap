Located in Etobicoke (Humber Valley Village), we offer music lessons to students of all ages and skill levels. The word “Da Capo” is an Italian musical term meaning “from the top”, indicating that a piece of music is to be played from the beginning. We thought this was a perfect name for our school, as our intent is to teach music to students “from the top”! Please explore our website to learn more about us. Have a question? Call us or fill out a “Contact Us” form and we will get back to you as possible. Interested in music lessons with us? Awesome!
